The Sunburst chart displays all urban areas in Vancouver in different color codes, with Vancouver being the main view in the main level. Once an area is clicked, the SunBurst graph switches to the second hierarchical level and displays all crimes within that area. If you select a crime with a mouse click, the third hierarchical level is displayed with all data of the crime (including date and time). Clicking on the date/time takes you to the fourth hierarchical level.
